Gnome Tower

Welcome to the alpha for Gnome Tower, the multiplayer VR voxel colony sim!
Join the Discord.

Download 0.0.34 (Windows)
Also available on Itch.

If it isn't already installed on your computer, you might need to additionally download the following MSVC++ redistributible (select vc_redist.x64.exe).


To play solo (Windows):
- Double click gnomes.exe!

To host a server (Windows):
- Enable port forwarding on your router
- Forward port 54000 (default) to your PC's local IP address
- Hit "Host Game" in gnomes.exe OR run gnome_server.exe

To play quest native (Quest- cannot act as host!):
- "Side load" gnometower-release.apk onto your quest (using adb or sidequest)
- Run gnome_server.exe on your PC
- Connect to your PC's local IP address (and port 54000 if using default)
- Warning: the quest version is in-progress, and may suffer performance issues
